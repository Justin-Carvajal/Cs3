Fun Cat Facts

  • Cats are carnivorous mammals, meaning they primarily eat meat.
  • They are domesticated descendants of the African wildcat.
  • Cats have retractable claws and are known for their agility and flexibility.
  • Cats have a grooming ritual to keep their fur clean and their skin healthy.
  • Cats are known for their independent nature and are often kept as pets.
